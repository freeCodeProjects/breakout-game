Breakout!

Rules:

Game has total 5 levels with increasing dificulty and you will have 5 chances to complete all 5 levels.
Use 'Left Arrow' or 'Z' key to move paddle left.
Use 'Right Arrow' or '/' key to move paddle right.
Use the paddle to bounce the ball up and break the bricks.
You can use spacebar to control play and pause of game.
